Our Mission

At VanMitra, our mission is to empower communities and authorities with advanced AI-powered tools for monitoring the Forest Rights Act (FRA) implementation. We aim to create transparency, support decision-making, and enhance sustainable forest governance across Madhya Pradesh, Tripura, Odisha, and Telangana. Our platform integrates geospatial mapping, decision support systems, and claim tracking to provide a holistic overview of forest rights and assets.
